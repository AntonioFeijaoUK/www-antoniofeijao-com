    aws-list-of-services.md

    Simple way to get a list of AWS Services. As of $ date +%Y-%B-%d, 2019-November-26 there are 206 service. Checked by using wc -c command at the end of the below script.

    curl --silent https://awspolicygen.s3.amazonaws.com/js/policies.js | sed '1s/[^=]*=//' | jq . | grep -B1 StringPrefix | grep -v StringPrefix | sed 's/\"//g;s/\-//g;s/: {//g' | grep -v "^$" | sort
